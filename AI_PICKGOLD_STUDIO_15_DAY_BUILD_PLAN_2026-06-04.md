## Goal

Use 15 days to turn AI PickGold Studio from a promising prototype into a clearer commercial website and product workbench across user, business, personal IP, product, content, and trust dimensions.

This is not only a visual redesign. The target is a reviewable and buildable system:

- Users know what the product does and how to start.
- The founder page makes High Soar credible and commercially legible.
- The business model is visible through service offers, Pro/API paths, and lead capture.
- The Studio workbench becomes easier to use, with saved runs and safer API boundaries.
- Linear tracks the work, while GitHub Pages stays the public proof surface.

## External Signals

- Creator AI adoption is now mainstream: 2026 creator reports and Adobe coverage indicate most creators already use generative AI, so differentiation cannot be "AI writes content"; it must be workflow, trust, packaging, and proof.
- Creator stacks are becoming SaaS-like: creators use multiple tools for scripts, image/video generation, captions, scheduling, analytics, and repurposing. AI PickGold should simplify the operating layer, not become another isolated generator.
- md2wechat-style products are moving toward agent/CLI/API ecosystems with themes, advanced modules, and draft APIs. AI PickGold must keep a clear safety boundary around credentials and manual publishing.
- Xiaohongshu/RedNote AI tools increasingly promise one-click topic, title, image, copy, and publish flows. AI PickGold should compete through cross-platform package quality, manual-publish trust, and personal-IP systemization rather than unsafe full automation.
- Platform and advertiser demand is shifting toward attribution, reporting, and measurable creator outcomes. Package history, lead capture, and review evidence are business-critical.

## Strategic Positioning

### Primary Position

AI PickGold Studio is a creator operating desk for turning one source draft into a reviewed, multi-platform publishing package.

### Founder IP Position

High Soar productizes AI workflows into systems that are reusable, reviewable, deliverable, and monetizable.

### Commercial Position

Free workbench proves the method. Paid value comes from package history, lead capture, private setup, md2wechat/API integration, and managed workflow implementation.

### Trust Position

AI helps package and review. The human still controls final account publishing. Credentials and platform APIs stay server-side.
